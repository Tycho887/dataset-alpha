# ETSI TS 119 132-2: Electronic Signatures and Infrastructures (ESI); XAdES digital signatures; Part 2: Extended XAdES signatures
**Source**: ETSI | **Version**: V1.0.1 | **Date**: 2015-07 | **Type**: Normative – Technical Specification
**Original**: http://www.etsi.org/standards-search (PDF)

## Scope (Summary)
Specifies additional XAdES signature levels (Extended XAdES) built on XML digital signatures by incorporating signed and unsigned qualifying properties for long-term validity. Addresses incremental requirements to maintain signature validity over time, with a higher degree of optionality than XAdES baseline signatures (ETSI TS 119 132‑1). Supports electronic signatures under Regulation (EU) No 910/2014.

## 4 Additional XAdES Levels Without References to Validation Data

### 4.1 Overview
Specifies additional XAdES levels generated by different combinations of XAdES qualifying properties (from TS 119 312‑1 [1]) using direct or indirect incorporation (clause 4.4 of [1]). Levels defined: XAdES‑E‑BES, XAdES‑E‑EPES, XAdES‑E‑T, and XAdES‑E‑A built on XAdES‑E‑T. Normative Annex A defines levels incorporating qualifying properties that encapsulate references to validation data and time‑stamps.

### 4.2 General Requirements
- XAdES qualifying properties obsoleted by ETSI TS 119 132‑1 [1] **shall not** appear in tables; their cardinality **shall** be 0 and they **shall not** be incorporated.
- Any XAdES signature of any level specified herein **shall** contain at least one of:
  - The `SigningCertificateV2` signed qualifying property, OR
  - The `ds:KeyInfo` element, with the following restrictions:
    - If `SigningCertificateV2` is absent, `ds:KeyInfo` **shall** include a `ds:X509Data` containing the signing certificate.
    - `ds:KeyInfo` **may** also contain other certificates.
    - The `ds:SignedInfo` **shall** contain a `ds:Reference` that ensures the signing certificate is actually signed.
  - *NOTE*: Signing the whole `ds:KeyInfo` locks the element; XPath transforms may be used to sign only the signing certificate.

**Additional Requirements (Table 1 notes):**

a) `SigningCertificateV2` **shall** be present if the signing certificate is not within `ds:KeyInfo` or if present but not signed by the signature; otherwise it **may** be absent.  
b) References to certificates in `SigningCertificateV2` **should not** include `IssuerSerialV2`.  
c) `SignaturePolicyStore` **may** be incorporated only if `SignaturePolicyIdentifier` is also present and contains `SigPolicyHash`; otherwise **shall not** be incorporated.  
d) Each `SignatureTimeStamp` **may** contain one or more electronic time‑stamps from different TSAs.  
e) Time‑stamps within `SignatureTimeStamp` **shall** be created before the signing certificate is revoked or expires.  
f) Duplication of certificate values in `CertificateValues` and `AttrAuthoritiesCertValues` **should** be avoided.  
g) For XAdES‑E‑A, incorporation of `CertificateValues` **shall** be determined by [1] clauses 5.5.2.2 and 5.5.2.3, step 4.a.  
h) For XAdES‑E‑A, incorporation of `AttrAuthoritiesCertValues` **shall** be determined by [1] clauses 5.5.2.2 and 5.5.2.3, step 4.c.  
i) Duplication of revocation values **should** be avoided.  
j) For XAdES‑E‑A, incorporation of `RevocationValues` **shall** be determined by [1] clauses 5.5.2.2 and 5.5.2.3, step 4.b.  
k) For XAdES‑E‑A, incorporation of `AttributeRevocationValues` **shall** be determined by [1] clauses 5.5.2.2 and 5.5.2.3, step 4.d.  
l) Validation data for electronic time‑stamps **shall** be present within `TimeStampValidationData` or embedded in the time‑stamp itself.  
m) Validation data **should** be included in the `TimeStampValidationData` qualifying property.  
n) Each `ArchiveTimeStamp` (namespace http://uri.etsi.org/01903/v1.4.1#) **may** contain more than one time‑stamp from different TSAs.  
o) XAdES‑E‑A signatures **shall** incorporate new archival time‑stamps in new `ArchiveTimeStamp` properties to a previously existing XAdES‑E‑A.  
p) Before generating a new `ArchiveTimeStamp`, all validation material required for validating the XAdES signature **shall** be included (certificates and status info for signing certificate, countersignatures, attribute certificates, and previous time‑stamps).  
q) `RenewedDigest` **should** be used when digest algorithms for external data objects in a `ds:Manifest` become weak.  
r) References to certificates in `CompleteCertificateRefsV2` and `AttributeCertificateRefsV2` **should not** include `IssuerSerialV2`.  
s) `AttributeCertificateRefsV2` **may** be used only if attribute certificates or signed assertions are incorporated; otherwise **shall not** be used.

**Notes (from clause 4.3):**
- Multiple instances of `SignatureTimeStamp`, `IndividualDataObjectsTimeStamp`, `AllDataObjectsTimeStamp`, `RefsOnlyTimeStampV2`, `SigAndRefsTimeStampV2` may be incorporated from different TSAs.
- For XAdES‑E‑BES, ‑E‑T, and ‑E‑A, the signature policy may be implicitly indicated.
- `SignaturePolicyIdentifier` can establish specific requirements for other qualifying properties.
- `SignatureTimeStamp` provides the initial steps towards long‑term validity.

- XAdES‑E‑C: built on XAdES‑E‑T by adding references to certificates and certificate status data.

- XAdES‑E‑X‑Long: built on XAdES‑E‑C by adding certificate and revocation values.

a) `CompleteCertificateRefsV2` and `AttributeCertificateRefsV2`: references **should not** include `IssuerSerialV2`.

i) Duplication of certificate values in `CertificateValues` and `AttrAuthoritiesCertValues` **should** be avoided.  
j) Duplication of revocation values **should** be avoided.
