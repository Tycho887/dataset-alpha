# ETSI TS 119 411-8: Policy and security requirements for Trust Service Providers issuing certificates; Part 8: Access Certificate Policy for EUDI Wallet Relying Parties
**Source**: ETSI | **Version**: V1.1.1 | **Date**: 2025-10 | **Type**: Normative
**Original**: DTS/ESI-0019411-8

## Scope (Summary)
This document specifies requirements for qualified and non-qualified certificates for electronic seals/signatures used as wallet-relying party access certificates, in accordance with eIDAS2 [i.2] and CIR (EU) 2025/848 [i.6]. It imposes no requirements on Competent Authorities.

## Definitions and Abbreviations
### Terms
- **competent authority**: authority recognized under applicable regulations as competent for national registration policies relating to EUDI wallet-relying parties
- **European digital identity wallet**: electronic identification means to securely store, manage and validate person identification data and electronic attestations of attributes for provision to relying parties (as defined in [i.2])
- **national register of wallet-relying parties**: national electronic register used by a Member State to make information on wallet-relying parties publicly available (as set out in Article 5b(5) of [i.1])
- **provider of wallet-relying party access certificates**: natural or legal person mandated by a Member State to issue wallet-relying party access certificates (as defined in [i.6])
- **registrar of wallet-relying parties**: body responsible for establishing and maintaining the list of registered wallet-relying parties designated by a Member State (as defined in [i.6])
- **wallet-relying party**: relying party that intends to rely upon wallet units for the provision of public or private services by means of digital interaction (as defined in [i.6])
- **wallet-relying party access certificate**: certificate for electronic seals or signatures authenticating and validating the wallet-relying party issued by a provider (as defined in [i.6])

### Abbreviations
- CRL: Certificate Revocation List
- eIDAS: electronic IDentification, Authentication and trust Services (Regulation (EU) No 910/2014)
- eIDAS2: Regulation (EU) 2024/1183 amending eIDAS
- EUDIW: European Digital Identity Wallet
- eudiwrp: european digital identity wallet relying party
- OCSP: Online Certificate Status Protocol
- TSP: Trust Service Provider (in context: provider of wallet-relying party access certificates)

### Notations
Requirements are identified by service component prefix (OVR, GEN, REG, REV, SDP, CSS) followed by clause number and incremental digit. Policy-specific markings: `[NCP-n-eudiwrp]`, `[NCP-l-eudiwrp]`, `[QCP-n-eudiwrp]`, `[QCP-l-eudiwrp]`.

### 6.6 Certificate, CRL, and OCSP Profiles
#### 6.6.1 Certificate Profile
- **GEN-6.6.1-01**: [3] clause 6.6.1 **shall** apply except those for website authentication certificates. NOTE: Neither website authentication nor short-term certificates are applicable.
- **GEN-6.6.1-02 [QCP-n-eudiwrp] [QCP-l-eudiwrp]**: [4] clause 6.6.1 **shall** apply.
- **GEN-6.6.1-03 [CHOICE]**: The certificate **shall** include at least one of the following policy identifiers: (for each policy type, the OID from clause 5.3 and/or an OID allocated by the TSP).
- **GEN-6.1.1-04**: For legal persons, the CommonName field **may** be a trade name or service name recognisable to the user.
- **GEN-6.6.1-05**: The organizationIdentifier field **shall** be present and encoded per ETSI EN 319 412-1 [1] clause 5.1.4, using ISO 3166-1 [2] country code "EL" for Greece.
- **GEN-6.6.1-06**: The cpsURI under Certificate policies **shall** indicate a URL where the CPS is.
- **GEN-6.6.1-07 [CHOICE]**: Contact information **shall** be at least one of: website (SAN uniformResourceIdentifier), phone number (SAN otherName with type-id id-at-telephoneNumber), or email (SAN rfc822Name).
- **GEN-6.6.1-08**: Different instances of the same organization **may** be differentiated using Organizational Units (OU).
- **GEN-6.6.1-09**: The CPS **shall** have contact information for the wallet-relying party regarding registration and intended use.
- **GEN-6.6.1-10**: Wallet-relying party access certificate attributes **shall** be derived from the register as specified in clause 5.1.2 of ETSI TS 119 475 [5].
