# ETSI TS 119 144-4 V1.1.1: PAdES digital signatures - Testing Conformance and Interoperability; Part 4: Testing Conformance of PAdES baseline signatures
**Source**: ETSI | **Version**: V1.1.1 | **Date**: June 2016 | **Type**: Normative Technical Specification
**Original**: http://www.etsi.org/standards-search

## Scope (Summary)
Defines a set of checks to test conformance of PAdES signatures against PAdES baseline signatures as specified in ETSI EN 319 142-1 [1]. It does not test technical validity of cryptographic material. Checks are limited to structural and element requirements from the PAdES specification. The document is technology‑agnostic and test assertions follow OASIS Test Assertions Guidelines [i.3].

## Normative References
- [1] ETSI EN 319 142-1: "Electronic Signatures and Infrastructures (ESI); PAdES digital signatures; Part 1: Building blocks and PAdES baseline signatures"
- [2] ETSI EN 319 142-2: "Electronic Signatures and Infrastructures (ESI); PAdES digital signatures; Part 2: Additional PAdES signatures profiles"

## Definitions and Abbreviations
- **Definitions**: Those given in ETSI TR 119 001 [i.2] apply.
- **Abbreviations**: Those given in ETSI TR 119 001 [i.2] apply.

## 4 Overview
ETSI EN 319 142-1 requirements are classified into:
1. **PAdES_BS**: requirements specific to PAdES baseline signatures (clauses 5 and 6 of [1]).
2. **PAdES_BB**: building block requirements applying to both baseline and additional PAdES signatures (clauses 4 and 5 of [1] and clauses 4 and 5 of [2]).
Tests address structure, values, interrelationships, and computations (e.g., message imprints). No tests on cryptographic validity. Each test assertion includes: unique identifier, normative source, target, optional prerequisite, predicate, prescription level (mandatory/recommended/permitted), and tag.
