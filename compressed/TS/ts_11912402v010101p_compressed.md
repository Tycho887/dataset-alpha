# ETSI TS 119 124-2 V1.1.1 (2016-06): CAdES digital signatures - Testing Conformance and Interoperability; Part 2: Test suites for testing interoperability of CAdES baseline signatures
**Source**: ETSI | **Version**: V1.1.1 | **Date**: June 2016 | **Type**: Normative (Technical Specification)
**Original**: [ETSI TS 119 124-2](https://www.etsi.org/deliver/etsi_ts/119100_119199/11912402/01.01.01_60/ts_11912402v010101p.pdf)

**Modal verbs terminology**: "shall", "shall not", "should", "should not", "may", "need not", "will", "will not", "can" and "cannot" are interpreted as per clause 3.2 of ETSI Drafting Rules. "must" is not allowed except in direct citation.

## Scope (Summary)
This document defines test suites to assess interoperability between implementations claiming conformance to CAdES baseline signatures [1]. Test suites cover four levels (B-B, B-T, B-LT, B-LTA), augmentation, and negative test cases. They are agnostic of PKI infrastructure.

## Definitions and Abbreviations
- **Negative test case**: test case for a signature whose validation according to ETSI EN 319 102-1 [i.3] would not result in TOTAL-PASSED.
- **Abbreviations**: those given in ETSI TR 119 001 [i.2] apply.

## Overview
ETSI EN 319 122-1 [1] defines four CAdES baseline signature levels. The test suites are layered accordingly, covering B-B, B-T, B-LT, B-LTA, augmentation, and negative test cases.

## 6 Testing interoperability of CAdES-B-T signatures
CAdES-B-T signatures shall be built on B-B conformant signatures. A B-T signature shall be a B-B signature with a trusted time-stamp token (SignatureTimeStamp) proving existence at a certain date/time. Mandatory attributes from [1] clause 6.3 shall be present.

| TC ID | Description | Pass criteria | Signature attributes |
|---|---|---|---|
| CAdES/BT/1 | Simplest CAdES-B-T: B-B mandatory plus SignatureTimeStamp. | Positive validation. The signature shall contain ContentType, SigningTime, MessageDigest, ESSSigningCertificateV2, SignatureTimeStamp and SigningCertificate attributes. | • Certificates: o SigningCertificate • SignedAttributes: o MessageDigest, o ESSSigningCertificateV2, o ContentType, o SigningTime • UnsignedAttributes: o SignatureTimeStamp |
| CAdES/BT/2 | Add independent B-T signature to an already signed document in B-T format. Input: CAdES/BT/1. | Positive validation. The signature shall contain 2 SigningCertificates attributes and 2 signerInfos including ContentType, SigningTime, MessageDigest, ESSSigningCertificateV2 and SignatureTimeStamp. | • Certificates: o SigningCertificate • SignedAttributes: o MessageDigest, o ESSSigningCertificateV2, o ContentType, o SigningTime • UnsignedAttributes: o SignatureTimeStamp |
| CAdES/BT/3 | Add independent B-T signature to a B-B signature (input CAdES/BB/1). | Positive validation. The signature shall contain 2 SigningCertificates attributes and 2 signerInfos: first with B-B attributes, second with additional SignatureTimeStamp. | • Certificates: o SigningCertificate • SignedAttributes: o MessageDigest, o ESSSigningCertificateV2, o ContentType, o SigningTime • UnsignedAttributes: o SignatureTimeStamp |

## 7 Testing interoperability of CAdES-B-LT signatures
CAdES-B-LT signatures shall be built on B-T signatures. A B-LT signature shall be a B-T signature with added certificates and certificate status values used for validation. Mandatory attributes from [1] clause 6.3 shall be present.

| TC ID | Description | Pass criteria | Signature attributes |
|---|---|---|---|
| CAdES/BLT/1 | Simplest B-LT: B-T mandatory plus CRLs (Crls.crl) as revocation data. | Positive validation. The signature shall contain ContentType, SigningTime, MessageDigest, Certificates, Crls.crl, ESSSigningCertificateV2 and SignatureTimeStamp attributes. | • Certificates • Crls.crl • SignedAttributes: o MessageDigest, o ESSSigningCertificateV2, o ContentType, o SigningTime • UnsignedAttributes: o SignatureTimeStamp |
| CAdES/BLT/2 | Similar to BLT/1 but using OCSP responses (Crls.other). | Positive validation. The signature shall contain ContentType, SigningTime, MessageDigest, Certificates, Crls.other, ESSSigningCertificateV2 and SignatureTimeStamp attributes. | • Certificates • Crls.other • SignedAttributes: o MessageDigest, o ESSSigningCertificateV2, o ContentType, o SigningTime • UnsignedAttributes: o SignatureTimeStamp |
| CAdES/BLT/3 | Includes CertifiedAttributeV2; CRLs used; includes Attribute Authority certificate. | Positive validation. The signature shall contain ContentType, SigningTime, MessageDigest, CertifiedAttributesV2 (in SignerAttributesV2), Certificates, Crls.crl, ESSSigningCertificateV2 and SignatureTimeStamp attributes. | • Certificates • Crls.crl • SignedAttributes: o MessageDigest, o ESSSigningCertificateV2, o ContentType, o SigningTime, o SignerAttributesV2 (CertifiedAttributeV2) • UnsignedAttributes: o SignatureTimeStamp |
| CAdES/BLT/4 | Includes CertifiedAttributeV2; OCSP responses; includes Attribute Authority certificate. | Positive validation. The signature shall contain ContentType, SigningTime, MessageDigest, CertifiedAttributesV2, Certificates, Crls.other, ESSSigningCertificateV2 and SignatureTimeStamp attributes. | • Certificates • Crls.other • SignedAttributes: o MessageDigest, o ESSSigningCertificateV2, o ContentType, o SigningTime, o SignerAttributesV2 (CertifiedAttributeV2) • UnsignedAttributes: o SignatureTimeStamp |

## 8 Testing interoperability of CAdES-B-LTA signatures
CAdES-B-LTA signatures shall be built on B-LT signatures. A B-LTA signature shall be a B-LT signature with one or more ArchiveTimeStampV3 attributes, each including one ats-hash-index-v3 unsigned attribute as specified in [1]. Mandatory attributes from [1] clause 6.3 shall be present.

| TC ID | Description | Pass criteria | Signature attributes |
|---|---|---|---|
| CAdES/BLTA/1 | Simplest B-LTA: B-LT with CRLs and one ArchiveTimeStampV3 (with ats-hash-index-v3). | Positive validation. Shall contain ContentType, SigningTime, MessageDigest, Certificates, Crls.crl, ESSSigningCertificateV2, SignatureTimeStamp and ArchiveTimeStampV3 (including one ats-hash-index-v3). | • Certificates • Crls.crl • SignedAttributes: o MessageDigest, o ESSSigningCertificateV2, o ContentType, o SigningTime • UnsignedAttributes: o SignatureTimeStamp, o ArchiveTimeStampV3, o ats-hash-index-v3 |
| CAdES/BLTA/2 | Like BLTA/1 but OCSP responses (Crls.other). | Positive validation. Shall contain ContentType, SigningTime, MessageDigest, Certificates, Crls.other, ESSSigningCertificateV2, SignatureTimeStamp and ArchiveTimeStampV3 (with ats-hash-index-v3). | • Certificates • Crls.other • SignedAttributes: o MessageDigest, o ESSSigningCertificateV2, o ContentType, o SigningTime • UnsignedAttributes: o SignatureTimeStamp, o ArchiveTimeStampV3, o ats-hash-index-v3 |
| CAdES/BLTA/3 | B-LT timestamped with ArchiveTimeStampV2, then with ArchiveTimeStampV3. Validation material for first ATSv2 added inside its token (TSU SignedData or certificate-values/revocation-values). CRLs used. | Positive validation. Shall contain ContentType, SigningTime, MessageDigest, Certificates, Crls.crl, CompleteCertificateRefs, CompleteRevocationRefs, ESSSigningCertificateV2, SignatureTimeStamp, ArchiveTimeStampV2 and ArchiveTimeStampV3 (with ats-hash-index-v3). | • Certificates • Crls.crl • SignedAttributes: o MessageDigest, o ESSSigningCertificateV2, o ContentType, o SigningTime • UnsignedAttributes: o CompleteCertificateRefs, o CompleteRevocationRefs, o SignatureTimeStamp, o ArchiveTimeStampV2, o Certificates (in token), o Crls.crl (in token), o ArchiveTimeStampV3, o ats-hash-index-v3 |
| CAdES/BLTA/4 | Like BLTA/3 but OCSP responses. | Positive validation. Same pass criteria but with Crls.other. | • Certificates • Crls.other • SignedAttributes: o MessageDigest, o ESSSigningCertificateV2, o ContentType, o SigningTime • UnsignedAttributes: o CompleteCertificateRefs, o CompleteRevocationRefs, o SignatureTimeStamp, o ArchiveTimeStampV2, o Certificates (in token), o Crls.other (in token), o ArchiveTimeStampV3, o ats-hash-index-v3 |
| CAdES/BLTA/5 | B-LT timestamped with ArchiveTimeStampV3, then another ArchiveTimeStampV3. First ATSv3 validation material in root SignedData. CRLs. | Positive validation. Shall contain ContentType, SigningTime, MessageDigest, Certificates, Crls.crl, ESSSigningCertificateV2, SignatureTimeStamp and 2 different ArchiveTimeStampV3 attributes. | • Certificates • Crls.crl • SignedAttributes: o MessageDigest, o ESSSigningCertificateV2, o ContentType, o SigningTime • UnsignedAttributes: o SignatureTimeStamp, o ArchiveTimeStampV3 (first), o ats-hash-index-v3 (first), o ArchiveTimeStampV3 (second), o ats-hash-index-v3 (second) |
| CAdES/BLTA/6 | Like BLTA/5 but OCSP responses. | Positive validation. Same pass criteria with Crls.other. | • Certificates • Crls.other • SignedAttributes: o MessageDigest, o ESSSigningCertificateV2, o ContentType, o SigningTime • UnsignedAttributes: o SignatureTimeStamp, o ArchiveTimeStampV3 (two), o ats-hash-index-v3 (two) |
| CAdES/BLTA/7 | B-LT includes explicit SignaturePolicyIdentifier; revocation data OCSP; one ArchiveTimeStampV3. | Positive validation. Shall contain ContentType, SigningTime, MessageDigest, SigningCertificate, SignaturePolicyIdentifier, Crls.crl, ESSSigningCertificateV2 and ArchiveTimeStampV3 (with ats-hash-index-v3). | • Certificates • Crls.crl • SignedAttributes: o MessageDigest, o ESSSigningCertificateV2, o ContentType, o SignaturePolicyIdentifier, o SigningTime • UnsignedAttributes: o SignatureTimeStamp, o ArchiveTimeStampV3, o ats-hash-index-v3 |

## 9 Testing CAdES baseline signatures augmentation interoperability
A CAdES-B-LTA signature shall be a B-T signature to which certificates, certificate status values, and one or more ArchiveTimeStampV3 (each with ats-hash-index-v3) have been added.

| TC ID | Description | Pass criteria | Signature attributes |
|---|---|---|---|
| CAdES/Aug/1 | Augment B-T signature to B-LTA: add validation material (CRLs for signer and timestamp certificate) and one ArchiveTimeStampV3. | Positive validation. The signature shall contain a B-T signature (ContentType, SigningTime, MessageDigest, ESSSigningCertificateV2, SignatureTimeStamp) to which Certificates, Crls.crl and ArchiveTimeStampV3 (with ats-hash-index-v3) have been added. | • CAdES-B-T signature with Certificates and Crls.crl • UnsignedAttributes: o ArchiveTimeStampV3, o ats-hash-index-v3 |
| CAdES/Aug/2 | Like Aug/1 but OCSP responses. | Positive validation. Same but with Crls.other. | • CAdES-B-T signature with Certificates and Crls.other • UnsignedAttributes: o ArchiveTimeStampV3, o ats-hash-index-v3 |
| CAdES/Aug/3 | Input is a B-LTA signature; augment by adding validation material for the ArchiveTimeStampV3 certificate (CRLs) and a second ArchiveTimeStampV3. | Positive validation. The signature shall contain a B-LTA signature (ContentType, SigningTime, MessageDigest, ESSSigningCertificateV2, SignatureTimeStamp, Certificates, Crls.crl, first ArchiveTimeStampV3) to which Certificates, Crls.crl and a second ArchiveTimeStampV3 (with ats-hash-index-v3) have been added. | • CAdES-B-T signature with Certificates and Crls.crl • UnsignedAttributes: o ArchiveTimeStampV3 (first), o ats-hash-index-v3 (first), o ArchiveTimeStampV3 (second), o ats-hash-index-v3 (second) |
| CAdES/Aug/4 | Like Aug/3 but OCSP responses. | Positive validation. Same but with Crls.other. | • CAdES-B-T signature with Certificates and Crls.other • UnsignedAttributes: two ArchiveTimeStampV3 with ats-hash-index-v3 |

## Requirements Summary

| ID | Requirement | Type | Reference |
|---|---|---|---|
| R1 | CAdES-B-B signatures shall contain mandatory attributes per [1] clause 6.3. | shall | Clause 5 |
| R2 | CAdES-B-T signatures shall be built on B-B signatures and include SignatureTimeStamp. | shall | Clause 6 |
| R3 | CAdES-B-LT signatures shall be built on B-T signatures and include certificates and revocation values. | shall | Clause 7 |
| R4 | CAdES-B-LTA signatures shall be built on B-LT signatures and include ArchiveTimeStampV3 with ats-hash-index-v3. | shall | Clause 8 |
| R5 | Augmentation from B-T to B-LTA shall add validation material and ArchiveTimeStampV3. | shall | Clause 9 |
| R6 | Negative test cases shall verify failure conditions per defined pass criteria. | shall | Clause 10 |
| R7 | All test cases are PKI-agnostic; any PKI including EU Trusted Lists may be used. | may | Clause 1 |

**Note**: The test case tables above contain the exact normative pass criteria and required signature attributes for each interoperability scenario.
