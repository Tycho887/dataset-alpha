# ETSI TS 119 144-3 V1.1.1: Electronic Signatures and Infrastructures (ESI); PAdES digital signatures - Testing Conformance and Interoperability; Part 3: Test suites for testing interoperability of additional PAdES signatures

**Source**: ETSI | **Version**: V1.1.1 | **Date**: 2016-06 | **Type**: Normative
**Original**: https://www.etsi.org/deliver/etsi_ts/119100_119199/11914403/01.01.01_60/ts_11914403v010101p.pdf

## Scope (Summary)

Defines test suites to assess interoperability between implementations claiming conformance to additional PAdES signatures profiles defined in ETSI EN 319 142-2 [3]. Test suites cover augmentation of additional PAdES signatures and negative test cases. PKI agnostic.

## 4 Testing CMS digital signatures in PDF interoperability

### 4.1 Introduction

Refers to clause 4 of ETSI EN 319 142-2 [3]. Tests different combinations of CMS digital signatures in PDF attributes based on ISO 32000-1 [1] and CMS.

**Requirement**: Mandatory attributes for CMS digital signatures in PDF described in ETSI EN 319 142-2 [3], clause 4.2, shall be present.

## 7 Testing interoperability of XAdES signatures signing XML content in PDF

### 7.1 Introduction

Refers to clause 6 of ETSI EN 319 142-2 [3]. Tests XAdES signatures within PDF containers.

### 7.2 Testing XAdES signatures of XML documents embedded in PDF containers

**Minimum requirement**: The xades:SigningCertificateV2 or the ds:KeyInfo element shall be used to secure the signing certificate.

Table 5: Test cases for XAdES signatures of XML documents embedded in PDF containers

| TC ID | Description | Pass criteria | Signature attributes |
|-------|-------------|---------------|----------------------|
| PAdES/XML/1 | XAdES-B-B with SignedSignatureProperties: SigningTime, SigningCertificateV2, SignatureProductionPlaceV2, SignerRoleV2. | Positive validation. Shall contain SignedInfo, SignatureValue, the signed properties listed. | • SignedInfo • SignatureValue • SignedSignatureProperties: SigningTime, SigningCertificateV2, SignatureProductionPlaceV2, SignerRoleV2 |
| PAdES/XML/2 | XAdES-B-T with SignedDataObjectProperties (CommitmentTypeIndication, AllDataObjectsTimeStamp) and unsigned property SignatureTimeStamp. | Positive validation. Shall contain SignedInfo, SignatureValue, SigningCertificateV2, CommitmentTypeIndication, AllDataObjectsTimeStamp, SignatureTimeStamp. | • SignedDataObjectProperties: CommitmentTypeIndication, AllDataObjectsTimeStamp • UnsignedSignatureProperties: SignatureTimeStamp |
| PAdES/XML/3 | XAdES-B-B with countersignature attribute. | Positive validation. Shall contain SignedInfo, SignatureValue, SigningCertificateV2, CounterSignature. | • UnsignedSignatureProperties: CounterSignature |
