# ETSI TS 103 090 V1.1.1: Conformity Assessment for Trust Service Providers issuing Extended Validation Certificates (EVC)
**Source**: ETSI Technical Committee ESI | **Version**: V1.1.1 | **Date**: 2012-04 | **Type**: Technical Specification (Normative)
**Original**: https://www.etsi.org/deliver/etsi_ts/103000_103099/103090/01.01.01_60/ts_103090v010101p.pdf

## Scope (Summary)
This document specifies requirements and guidance for the supervision and assessment of a Trust Service Provider (TSP) issuing Extended Validation Certificates (EVC) through audit against TS 102 042 [i.1]. It references general requirements from TS 119 403 [1] and adds further requirements as appropriate to EVC.

## Definitions and Abbreviations
- **Definitions**: Terms and definitions given in TS 102 042 [i.1] and TS 119 403 [1] apply.
- **Abbreviations**: Those given in TS 102 042 [i.1] and TS 119 403 [1] apply.

## 5 Assessment Process
- **General**: The assessment of the TSP issuing EVC shall be carried out as specified in clause 5 of TS 119 403 [1].
- **Checklist**: It is recommended that the checklist used for the assessment is based on TR 101 564 [i.2], Annex A.
- **Audit report**: It is recommended that the assessor produces an audit report addressing the topics identified in TR 101 564 [i.2], Annex B.

### 5.1 Additional audit requirements for EVC
- **Requirement**: Before issuing EVCs, the TSP shall have a currently valid TS 102 042 [i.1] certification and then complete a point-in-time readiness audit against TS 102 042 [i.1] with the EVC conformant if needed (per section 14.1.1 of EVCG [i.3]).
- **Self-declaration**: The TSP can use the checklist of TR 101 564 [i.2], Annex A as a basis for self-declaration.
- **Compliance**: These requirements shall be in accordance with clause 5.3 of TS 119 403 [1].

### 5.2 Publication of the Assessment report
- The assessment report is provided to the Notification Body.
- The report may be provided to the browsers or application software providers by the TSP no later than three months after the end of the audit period (per section 14.1.3(3) of EVCG [i.3]).
- In the event of a delay greater than three months, the TSP shall provide an explanation signed by the auditing body if requested.

### 5.3 Regular Surveillance activities
- The Notification Body and the TSP should define a programme of periodic surveillance and reassessment at sufficiently close intervals to verify that TSPs continue to comply with the requirements.
- This programme should meet the requirements of clause 5.4 of TS 119 403 [1] and section 14.1.2 of EVCG [i.3].

### 5.4 Incidents handling
- The TSP shall inform the Notification Body of all relevant information without unnecessary delay and follow the requirements of clause 5.5 of TS 119 403 [1].
- It is also recommended to notify the browsers or application software vendors.

### 5.5 Reassessment
- The TSP audit shall follow the requirements of clause 5.6 of TS 119 403 [1].

## 6 Requirements on TSP conformity assessment body
- The Conformity Assessment Body shall meet the requirements specified in clause 6 of TS 119 403 [1].

### 6.1 Competence criteria and qualification
- To ensure the team of assessors has all necessary expertise, they shall meet the requirements of clause 6.2 of TS 119 403 [1] and section 14.1.4 of EVCG [i.3].

## 7 Cross Border Assessments
- The TSP audit shall meet the requirements specified in clause 7 of TS 119 403 [1].

## 4 Introduction (Informative – Condensed)
The Conformity Assessment for EVC applies the general system for TSP Accreditation as illustrated in Figure 1 (omitted). The scheme involves European co-operation for Accreditation (EA), National Accreditation Bodies, Notification Bodies, and Conformity Assessors. Trust Service Providers are assessed against criteria defined in TS 102 042 and the EVC Guidelines. The document builds on TS 119 403.

## Annex A (Informative): Self-declaration
The TSP is recommended to provide a self-declaration of compliance against supervision criteria of TS 102 042 [i.1] at the initiation of supervision. The self-declaration can be based on the checklist in Annex A of TR 101 564 [i.2]. The Notification Body is responsible for implementing its supervision system and may take corrective actions if the TSP fails to comply. Self-declaration is not considered evidence of conformity for CAB Forum EV guidelines.
