# ETSI TS 119 534-1: Electronic Signatures and Infrastructures (ESI); Testing Conformance and Interoperability of Registered Electronic Mail Services; Part 1: Testing conformance
**Source**: ETSI TC ESI | **Version**: V1.2.1 | **Date**: 2023-12 | **Type**: Normative Technical Specification
**Original**: https://www.etsi.org/deliver/etsi_ts/119500_119599/11953401/01.02.01_60/ts_11953401v010201p.pdf

## Scope (Summary)
Defines checks for testing conformance of Registered Electronic Mail Services (REMS) against ETSI EN 319 532-3 [1] and ETSI EN 319 532-1 [2] requirements, covering MIME/SMIME headers and bodies, REM message structures, digital signatures, Common Service Interface aspects, and ERDS evidence. Does not test conformance against underlying technical specifications.

### 5.3 Signed data MIME section header
- **Content-Type**: shall be "multipart/mixed" (Mandatory). 'boundary' should be conformant to RFC 2046 (Recommended). Presence mandatory.

### 5.4 REMS introduction MIME section header
- **REM-Section-Type**: should be "rem_message/introduction" (Recommended); presence Optional.
- **Content-Type**: shall be "multipart/alternative" (Mandatory). 'boundary' should be conformant to RFC 2046 (Recommended). Presence mandatory.

#### 5.4.2 Free text MIME subsection header
- **Content-Type**: shall be "text/plain" (Mandatory). 'charset' should be "UTF-8" (Recommended). Presence mandatory.
- **Content-Disposition**: shall be "inline" (Mandatory). Presence Optional.
- **Content-Transfer-Encoding**: should be "7bit", "8bit", or "quoted-printable" (Recommended). Presence mandatory.

#### 5.4.3 HTML MIME subsection header
- **Content-Type**: shall be "text/html" (Mandatory). 'charset' should be "UTF-8" (Recommended). Presence mandatory.
- **Content-Transfer-Encoding**: should be "7bit", "8bit", or "quoted-printable" (Recommended). Presence mandatory.

### 5.5 Original message MIME section header
- **Content-Type**: shall be "message/rfc822"; 'name' parameter shall be "AttachedMimeMessage" (Mandatory). Presence mandatory.
- **Content-Transfer-Encoding**: shall be "binary" (Mandatory). Presence mandatory.
- **Content-Disposition**: shall be "attachment"; 'filename' shall match 'name' parameter of Content-Type (Mandatory). Presence mandatory.
- **Content-Description**: presence Optional.
- **REM-Section-Type**: should be "rem_message/original" (Recommended); presence Optional.
- Changes to original headers: only if strictly necessary; changes shall be clearly indicated to sender and recipient (Mandatory).

### 5.6 REMS extension MIME section header
- **Content-Type**: should be "application/xml", "application/octet-stream", or "message/rfc822" (Recommended). 'name' and 'charset' recommendations. Presence mandatory.
- **Content-Transfer-Encoding**: shall be "quoted-printable", "base64", or "binary" (Mandatory). Presence Recommended.
- **Content-Disposition**: shall be "attachment"; 'filename' shall match 'name' (Mandatory). Presence mandatory.
- **Content-Description**: presence Optional.
- **REM-Section-Type**: should be "rem_message/extension" (Recommended); presence Optional.
- **REM-Extension-Code**: should be a unique code (Recommended); presence Optional.
- **REM-Extension-Namespace-URI**: should contain the namespace URI (Recommended); presence Optional.
- When containing structured metadata/evidence: REM-Extension-Code shall contain component code from Table 5/6 of [8] (Mandatory); 'name' parameter shall be based on component name (Mandatory); REM-Extension-Namespace-URI shall contain target namespace URI (Mandatory).

### 5.7 ERDS evidence MIME section header

#### 5.7.1 XML ERDS evidence
- **Content-Type**: shall be "application/xml"; 'name' shall be "<REM_EVIDENCE_NAME>.xml"; 'charset' shall be "UTF-8" (Mandatory). Presence mandatory.
- **Content-Transfer-Encoding**: shall be "quoted-printable", "base64", or "binary" (Mandatory). Presence mandatory.
- **Content-Disposition**: shall be "attachment"; 'filename' shall match 'name' (Mandatory). Presence mandatory.
- **Content-Description**: presence Optional.
- **REM-Section-Type**: should be "rem_message/xml_evidence" (Recommended); presence Optional.

#### 5.7.2 PDF ERDS evidence
- **Content-Type**: shall be "application/pdf"; 'name' shall be "<REM_EVIDENCE_NAME>.pdf" (Mandatory). Presence mandatory.
- **Content-Transfer-Encoding**: shall be "base64" or "binary" (Mandatory). Presence mandatory.
- **Content-Disposition**: shall be "attachment"; 'filename' shall match 'name' (Mandatory). Presence mandatory.
- **Content-Description**: presence Optional.
- **REM-Section-Type**: should be "rem_message/pdf_evidence" (Recommended); presence Optional.

### 5.8 REMS signature MIME section header
- **Content-Type**: shall be "application/pkcs7-signature; name=smime.p7s" (Mandatory). 'name' parameter should be present (Mandatory in REM Baseline). Presence mandatory.
- **Content-Transfer-Encoding**: shall be "base64" (Mandatory). Presence Recommended.
- **Content-Disposition**: shall be "attachment"; 'filename' should be "smime.p7s" (Mandatory; in REM Baseline 'filename' shall be "smime.p7s"). Presence mandatory.
- **Content-Description**: may be "S/MIME Cryptographic Signature" (Optional). Presence Optional.

## 6 Test assertions for MIME sections bodies

### 6.2 Introduction MIME section body
- If both free text and HTML sections present, contents shall be alternative and equivalent (Mandatory).
- HTML body shall not contain active code (Mandatory).
- If URL present, printed part shall be same as hidden part (Mandatory).

## 7 Test assertions for REM messages structures

### 7.2 REM payload structure
Mandatory components: outermost MIME section, signed data MIME section, Introduction MIME section (with both free text and HTML subsections), Original message MIME section, REMS signature MIME section. Extension MIME sections optional.

### 7.3 REMS notification structure
Mandatory: outermost MIME section, signed data MIME section, Introduction MIME section (with free text and HTML), REMS signature MIME section. ERDS Evidence MIME sections and extension sections optional.

### 7.4 REMS receipt structure
Mandatory: outermost MIME section, signed data MIME section, Introduction MIME section (with free text and HTML), one or more ERDS Evidence MIME sections, REMS signature MIME section. Extension sections optional.

### 7.5 REM dispatch structure
Mandatory: outermost MIME section, signed data MIME section, Introduction MIME section (with free text and HTML), Original message MIME section, one or more ERDS Evidence MIME sections, REMS signature MIME section. Extension sections optional.

## 8 Test assertions for signatures

### 8.2 Signatures individually signing ERDS evidence
- Algorithms and key lengths should be as per ETSI TS 119 312 [i.1] (Recommended).
- Signatures generated by REMS or delegated entity (Optional).
- For XML evidence, signature shall be XAdES compliant with [8] clause 7.2 and [9] clause 5.2.2.28 (Mandatory).
- For PDF evidence, should be PAdES per [i.4] (Recommended).

### 8.3 Signatures signing REM messages
- Algorithms and key lengths should be per [i.1] (Recommended).
- Signature shall be applied using S/MIME multipart/signed as per RFC 8551 [3] (Mandatory).
- Signature shall protect all MIME parts (Mandatory).
- Should be CAdES baseline signature per [i.5] (Recommended).
- May include signed attribute signature-policy-identifier (Optional).
- Should be augmented to CAdES-B-T (Recommended).
- Signing certificate shall meet requirements of [8] clause 9.3 (Mandatory).

## 9 Test assertions for Common Service Interface

### 9.2 Routing information
- REM RI should be identified by transport protocol, hostname, port (Recommended).
- Other techniques may be used (Optional).

### 9.3 Trust information binding
- If TL used, section shall conform to ETSI TS 119 612 [10] (Mandatory).
- Service type identifier mandatory, one of: http://uri.etsi.org/TrstSvc/Svctype/EDS/REM or .../REM/Q for qualified.
- Service digital identity mandatory: shall contain one X.509 certificate (either REMS signing cert or CA cert for signing components); may contain X509SKI.
- Service supply point mandatory (one or more URIs to REM RI).
- TSP service definition URI optional.
- Service information extensions optional; if present, shall not be set as critical.

### 9.4 Capability discovery and management
- If TL used, capability metadata may be published via TL (Mandatory when used).
- TSP service definition URI optional; may contain URI for downloading capability metadata.
- Service information extensions optional; additionalServiceInformation may contain URI or embed XML metadata.
- ServiceSupplyPoint may contain URIs for capability and security metadata.

## 10 Test assertions for ERDS evidence structure and content
Test assertions from ETSI TS 119 524-1 [7] clause 6 shall apply.

## 11 Test assertions for provision of the service

### 11.2 Generation of ERDS evidence in response to events
Mandatory evidence generation:
- SubmissionAcceptance after acceptance of user content.
- SubmissionRejection after rejection (with reason).
- RelayAcceptance after acceptance of relaying request.
- RelayRejection after rejection of relaying (with reason).
- RelayFailure after failure to relay.
- ContentConsignment after successful consignment.
- ContentConsignmentFailure after unsuccessful consignment.
- ContentHandover (conditional) after successful handover (if no consignment attempted).
- ContentHandoverFailure (conditional) after unsuccessful handover.
- ConsignmentAcceptance (S&N style) after recipient acceptance.
- ConsignmentRejection (S&N style) after recipient rejection.
- AcceptanceRejectionExpiry (S&N style) after expiry without reaction.

Recommended: NotificationForAcceptance, NotificationForAcceptanceFailure, RelayToNonERDS, RelayToNonERDSFailure, ReceivedFromNonERDS.
Optional: ConsignmentNotification, ConsignmentNotificationFailure.

### 11.3 Test assertions on REM interfaces

#### 11.3.2 Security measures
- MRI (Message Retrieval Interface) shall require identification and authentication, confidentiality, integrity (Mandatory).
- ERI (Evidence Retrieval Interface) shall require identification and authentication, confidentiality, integrity (Mandatory).
- Other interfaces (MSI, RI) see [7] and the present document.

#### 11.3.3 Provision of REM interfaces
- MSI should be provided using SMTP over TLS (Recommended); other protocols allowed if secure channel (Optional).
- MRI shall be provided (Mandatory); should use IMAP over TLS or POP3 over TLS (Recommended); other protocols allowed (Optional).
- ERI shall be provided (Mandatory); may use same or separate channel (Optional).
- REMS may provide multiple REM RI (Optional).
- RI should be provided using SMTP over TLS (Recommended); other protocols allowed (Optional); should follow [11] clause 5.
- ARI shall be provided when REMS operates in S&N style (Mandatory).

## 12 Test assertions for REM Baseline

### 12.1 Introduction
REM Baseline conformance requires all test assertions from clauses 5-11 except those explicitly superseded or suppressed (see original document for full list). Additional specific test assertions are defined below.

### 12.2 SMIME sections headers

#### 12.2.2 REMS Relay metadata header (REM Baseline additions)
- REM-ExpirationDate shall not be present (Mandatory)
- REM-ScheduledDelivery shall not be present (Mandatory)
- REM-MD08 header shall be present (Mandatory)
- Signature header shall be present (Mandatory)
- REM-Evidence-ID shall contain G01 component of EvidenceIdentifier (Mandatory)
- REM-Evidence-ID should be mapped as Message-ID format (Recommended)

#### 12.2.4 REMS introduction MIME section header (REM Baseline)
- REM-Section-Type shall be present and have value "rem_message/introduction" (Mandatory)
- Test assertions REMS/HEADER/INTRO/FIELD/TA_01 and TA_02 shall not apply.

#### 12.2.5 Original message MIME section header (REM Baseline)
- REM-Section-Type shall have value "rem_message/original" and be present (Mandatory)

#### 12.2.6 Extension MIME section header (REM Baseline)
- REM-Section-Type shall have value "rem_message/extension" and be present (Mandatory)

#### 12.2.7 ERDS evidence MIME section header (REM Baseline)
- REM-Section-Type shall have value "rem_message/xml_evidence" and be present (Mandatory)

#### 12.2.8 REMS signature MIME section header (REM Baseline)
- 'name' parameter of Content-Type shall be present (Mandatory)
- 'filename' parameter of Content-Disposition shall have value "smime.p7s" (Mandatory)

### 12.5 Interfaces (REM Baseline)

#### 12.5.2 MSI
- Protocol shall secure communication, ensure identification, authentication, confidentiality, authenticity, integrity (Mandatory)

#### 12.5.3 MERI
- Protocol shall secure communication, ensure identification, authentication, confidentiality, authenticity, integrity (Mandatory)

#### 12.5.4 RI
- Shall be implemented using SMTP over TLS per RFC 3207 [15] (Mandatory)

#### 12.5.5 CSI

##### 12.5.5.2 Routing information
- Routing interface shall use DNS protocol properly secured (Mandatory)
- Hostname of REM RI shall be available in MX records and match ServiceSupplyPoint URI (Mandatory)

##### 12.5.5.3 Trust information binding
- Trusting interface shall use Trusted List (Mandatory); for qualified REM, EU TL (Mandatory)
- Service digital identity shall contain X509Certificate element (Mandatory); optionally X509SubjectName and X509SKI.
- ServiceSupplyPoints shall contain two entries: SMTP server URI and capability metadata URI (Mandatory)

##### 12.5.5.4 Capability discovery and management
- Common capabilities shall be referenced in TL via URI in ServiceSupplyPoint (Mandatory)
- CapabilityAndSecurityInformation element structure: SchemeData, CapabilityMetadata, SecurityMetadata, ds:Signature? (Mandatory)
- Version attribute of CapabilityAndSecurityInformation shall be "EN319532v1" (Mandatory)
- ERDSMetadata version attribute shall be "EN319522v1.1.1" (Mandatory)
- ERDSDomain shall match ServiceSupplyPoint without scheme/port (Mandatory)
- ERDSGoverningBody shall equal English TSPName (Mandatory)
- ERDSProfileSupported shall be "http://uri.etsi.org/19532/v1#/REMbaseline" (Mandatory)
- ERDSExpiryDateAndTimeSupport, ERDSScheduledDeliverySupport shall be false (Mandatory)
- ERDSAssuranceLevelsSupported shall be "http://eidas.europa.eu/LoA/substantial" (Mandatory)
- ERDSSupportedConsignmentModes shall be "http://uri.etsi.org/19522/v1#/consignment/basic" (Mandatory)
- SecurityMetadata/CapabilityBasedSecurity version "EN319532v1"; TLSCertificate contains base64 DER-encoded X.509 cert used for TLS (Mandatory)
- DomainSigningCertificate optional; ds:Signature shall be XAdES-B-B, optionally XAdES-B-T (Mandatory/Optional)
