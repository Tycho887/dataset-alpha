# ETSI TS 119 124-5 V1.1.1: Electronic Signatures and Infrastructures (ESI); CAdES digital signatures - Testing Conformance and Interoperability; Part 5: Testing conformance of extended CAdES signatures
**Source**: ETSI | **Version**: V1.1.1 | **Date**: 2016-06 | **Type**: Normative Technical Specification
**Original**: http://www.etsi.org/standards-search (DTS/ESI-0019124-5)

## Scope (Summary)
Defines checks for conformance testing of CAdES signatures against extended CAdES signatures as per ETSI EN 319 122-2 [2]. Only checks specific to extended signatures are defined; common checks with baseline signatures are in ETSI TS 119 124-4 [3]. The document does not specify checks for technical validity of cryptographic material. Tests cover structural requirements, element values, interrelationships, and computed message imprints using test assertions following OASIS guidelines.

## 4 Overview (Informative - Condensed)
Classifies requirements from EN 319 122-1 [1] and EN 319 122-2 [2] into:
- **CAdES_BB**: Requirements from clauses 4, 5, and Annex A of [1] common to baseline and extended signatures – checks defined in [3].
- **CAdES_ES**: Requirements from clauses 5 and 6 of [2] specific to extended signatures.

Tests are structured as test assertions per [i.3], including: unique identifier (starting "CAdES_ES"), normative source, target, optional prerequisite, predicate, prescription level (mandatory/recommended/permitted), and tag. No tests verify actual validity of cryptographic material. Signature levels considered: CAdES-E-BES, CAdES-E-EPES, CAdES-E-T, CAdES-E-A.

## 5 Testing conformance to CAdES-E-BES extended signatures
### 5.1 Introduction
This clause specifies assertions for signatures claiming conformance to CAdES-E-BES per [2]. All constraints from [2] on CMS Signature elements are tested. Each subclause either references checks from [3] or defines specific assertions.

### 5.2 Testing CMSversion
- The CAdES_BB/CMSV check in [3] shall apply.

### 5.3 Testing DigestAlgorithmIdentifiers
- The CAdES_BB/DAI check in [3] shall apply.

### 5.4 Testing EncapsulatedContentInfo
- The CAdES_BB/ECI check in [3] shall apply.

### 5.5 Testing SignedData.certificates
- The CAdES_BB/SDC/1 and 2 checks in [3] shall apply.

### 5.6 Testing SignedData.crls
- The CAdES_BB/SDCRL/1, 2 and 3 checks in [3] shall apply.

### 5.7 Testing SignerInfos
- The CAdES_BB/SI/1 check in [3] shall apply.

### 5.8 Testing content-type
- The CAdES_BB/CTY/1 check in [3] shall apply.

### 5.9 Testing message-digest
- The CAdES_BB/MD/1 check in [3] shall apply.

### 5.10 Testing signing-time
- TA id: CAdES_ES/STI/1
- Normative source: [2] - Clause 4.3
- Target: CAdES signature generator claiming conformance to CAdES-E-BES format as specified in [2]
- Predicate: For new signatures, applications include SigningTime attribute in CAdES signature.
- Prescription level: permitted
- Tag: CAdES-E-BES signatures.
- The CAdES_BB/STI/1 check in [3] shall apply.

### 5.13 Testing signer-location

### 5.15 Testing counter-signature

### 5.16 Testing content-time-stamp

## 6 Testing conformance to CAdES-E-EPES extended signatures
### 6.1 Introduction
CAdES-E-EPES signatures are built on CAdES-E-BES. They shall fulfill requirements of clause 5 and clauses 6.2, 6.3.

### 6.2 Testing signature-policy-identifier
- TA id: CAdES_ES/SPID/1
- Normative source: [2] - Clause 4.3
- Target: CAdES signature generator claiming conformance to CAdES-E-EPES format as specified in [2]
- Predicate: For new signatures, applications include signature-policy-identifier attribute in CAdES signature.
- Prescription level: mandatory
- Tag: CAdES-E-EPES signatures.

### 6.3 Testing signature-policy-store
- The CAdES_BB/SPS/1 check in [3] shall apply.

## 7 Testing conformance to CAdES-E-T extended signatures
### 7.1 Introduction
CAdES-E-T signatures are built on CAdES-E-BES. They shall fulfill requirements of clause 5 and clause 7.2.

### 7.2 Testing SignatureTimeStamp

### 8.4 Testing Certificate and Revocation values
- TA id: CAdES_ES/CER/1
- Normative source: [2] - Clause 4.3
- Target: CAdES signature generator claiming conformance to CAdES-E-A format as specified in [2]
- Predicate: For new signatures, applications include the full set of certificates, including the trust anchors when they are available in the form of certificates, that have been used to validate the CAdES signature.
- Prescription level: mandatory
- Tag: CAdES-E-A signatures.
- TA id: CAdES_ES/REV/1
- Normative source: [2] - Clause 4.3
- Target: CAdES signature generator claiming conformance to CAdES-E-A format as specified in [2]
- Predicate: For new signatures, applications include the full set of revocation data (CRL or OCSP responses) that have been used to validate the CAdES signature.
- Prescription level: mandatory
- Tag: CAdES-E-A signatures.
- TA id: CAdES_ES/CV/1
- Normative source: [2] - Clause 4.3
- Target: CAdES signature generator claiming conformance to CAdES-E-A format as specified in [2]
- Predicate: For new signatures, applications include certificate-values attribute in CAdES signature.
- Prescription level: permitted
- Tag: Extended CAdES signatures.
- TA id: CAdES_ES/RV/1
- Normative source: [2] - Clause 4.3
- Target: CAdES signature generator claiming conformance to CAdES-E-A format as specified in [2]
- Predicate: For new signatures, applications include revocation-values attribute in CAdES signature.
- Prescription level: permitted
- Tag: Extended CAdES signatures.
- TA id: CAdES_ES/CV/2
- Normative source: [3] - Clause 4.3
- Target: CAdES signature generator claiming conformance to CAdES signatures as specified in [2]
- Predicate: For new signatures, applications avoid duplication of certificate values in the certificate-values attribute in CAdES signature.
- Prescription level: recommended
- Tag: Extended CAdES signatures.
- TA id: CAdES_ES/RV/2
- Normative source: [3] - Clause 4.3
- Target: CAdES signature generator claiming conformance to CAdES signatures as specified in [2]
- Predicate: For new signatures, applications avoid duplication of revocation values in the revocation-values attribute in CAdES signature.
- Prescription level: recommended
- Tag: Extended CAdES signatures.
