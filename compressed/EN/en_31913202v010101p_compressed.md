# ETSI EN 319 132-2: Electronic Signatures and Infrastructures (ESI); XAdES digital signatures; Part 2: Extended XAdES signatures
**Source**: ETSI/ESI | **Version**: V1.1.1 | **Date**: 2016-04 | **Type**: Normative
**Original**: [ETSI EN 319 132-2 V1.1.1 (2016-04)](http://www.etsi.org/standards-search)

## Scope (Summary)
This document specifies extended XAdES digital signature levels (XAdES-E) built on XML digital signatures [i.4] by incorporating signed and unsigned qualifying properties to maintain long-term validity. It covers a hierarchy of incremental levels from XAdES-E-BES to XAdES-E-A, offering greater optionality than baseline signatures defined in ETSI EN 319 132‑1 [1]. Procedures for creation, augmentation, and validation are out of scope (see ETSI EN 319 102‑1 [i.7]).

## 4 Additional XAdES levels without references to validation data

### 4.1 Overview
Defines four levels: XAdES-E-BES, XAdES-E-EPES, XAdES-E-T, and XAdES-E-A built on XAdES-E-T. Levels are generated by combinations of qualifying properties from [1], using direct or indirect incorporation (clause 4.4 of [1]). Normative Annex A defines levels with references to validation data.

### 4.2 General requirements
- **Deprecated properties**: Cardinality shall be 0; they shall not be incorporated. (EN 319 132‑1 Annex D)
- **SigningCertificateV2 or ds:KeyInfo**: Any XAdES signature shall contain at least one of:
  - `SigningCertificateV2` signed qualifying property.
  - `ds:KeyInfo` element, with restrictions:
    - If `SigningCertificateV2` is absent, `ds:KeyInfo` shall include a `ds:X509Data` containing the signing certificate.
    - `ds:KeyInfo` may also contain other certificates.
    - `ds:SignedInfo` shall contain a `ds:Reference` that ensures the signing certificate is signed. (NOTE 1: Signing whole `ds:KeyInfo` locks it; XPath transforms may be used.)
- **Algorithms and key lengths**: should be as specified in ETSI TS 119 312 [i.8]; national recommendations may supersede. (NOTE 2, NOTE 3)

### 4.3 XAdES-E-BES, XAdES-E-EPES, XAdES-E-T, and XAdES-E-A built on XAdES-E-T
- **XAdES-E-EPES** built on XAdES-E-BES by adding one `SignaturePolicyIdentifier`.
- **XAdES-E-T** built on XAdES-E-BES or XAdES-E-EPES by adding one or more `SignatureTimeStamp`.
- **XAdES-E-A** built on XAdES-E-T (or levels from Annex A) by adding one or more `ArchiveTimeStamp`.

- p) Before adding a new `ArchiveTimeStamp`, all validation material for the signature shall be included (certificates and status info for signer, countersignatures, attribute certs, previous time‑stamps).

- r) References in `CompleteCertificateRefsV2` and `AttributeCertificateRefsV2` should not include `IssuerSerialV2`.
- s) `AttributeCertificateRefsV2` and `AttributeRevocationRefs` may be used when at least one attribute certificate or signed assertion is incorporated; otherwise shall not.

## 5 Legacy signatures
When new qualifying properties are incorporated to legacy XAdES TS 101 903 [i.3] signatures, these qualifying properties shall comply with ETSI EN 319 132‑1 [1].

- a) References in `CompleteCertificateRefsV2` and `AttributeCertificateRefsV2` should not include `IssuerSerialV2`.
- b) XAdES-E-C and higher levels **shall not** be generated in case of direct trust (trust anchor public key in signing certificate). (See NOTE)
- c) `AttributeCertificateRefsV2` may be incorporated only if attribute certificates or signed assertions are in `SignerRoleV2`; otherwise shall not.
- d) `AttributeRevocationRefs` may be incorporated only if attribute certificates or signed assertions are in `SignerRoleV2`; otherwise shall not.
- e) `CertificateValues` shall be incorporated if signature misses required certificates [1] 5.4.1; otherwise shall not.
- f) `RevocationValues` shall be incorporated if signature misses required revocation data [1] 5.4.2; otherwise shall not.
- g) `AttrAuthoritiesCertValues` shall be incorporated if attribute cert/signed assertion present and validation certificates missing from `ds:KeyInfo` or `CertificateValues`; otherwise shall not.
- h) `AttributeRevocationValues` shall be incorporated if attribute cert/signed assertion present and revocation data missing from `ds:KeyInfo` or `RevocationValues`; otherwise shall not.
- i) Duplication of certificate values should be avoided.
- j) Duplication of revocation values should be avoided.
- NOTE: In direct trust, `CompleteCertificateRefsV2` and `CompleteRevocationRefs` would contain no references.
