# ETSI TR 119 124-1 V1.1.1: CAdES digital signatures - Testing Conformance and Interoperability; Part 1: Overview
**Source**: ETSI | **Version**: V1.1.1 | **Date**: 2016-06 | **Type**: Informative
**Original**: http://www.etsi.org/standards-search

## Scope (Summary)
This Technical Report provides an overview of the multi-part deliverable covering test suites for testing interoperability and conformance of CAdES signatures (Parts 2–5). It describes each part’s coverage and lists the features of each test suite. This document is for information only; normative requirements are in other parts.

## Definitions and Abbreviations
- **negative test case**: test case for a signature whose validation according to ETSI EN 319 102-1 [i.5] would not result in TOTAL-PASSED
- **Abbreviations**: As given in ETSI TR 119 001 [i.3].

## Testing Conformance and Interoperability

### Part 2: Test suites for testing interoperability of CAdES baseline signatures
- **Reference**: ETSI TS 119 124-2 [i.6]
- **Basis**: Against ETSI EN 319 122-1 [i.1]
- **Levels covered**: B-B, B-T, B-LT, B-LTA
- **Test layers**:
  - Interoperability at B-B, B-T, B-LT, B-LTA levels
  - Augmentation from B-T to B-LTA
  - Negative CAdES baseline signatures (B-B, B-T, B-LTA test cases)

### Part 3: Test suites for testing interoperability of extended CAdES signatures
- **Reference**: ETSI TS 119 124-3 [i.7]
- **Basis**: Against ETSI EN 319 122-2 [i.2]
- **Forms covered**: CAdES-E-BES, CAdES-E-EPES, CAdES-E-T, CAdES-E-C, CAdES-E-X, CAdES-E-X Long, CAdES-E-A (with ATSv2 and ATSv3)
- **Test layers**:
  - Interoperability for each form
  - Negative extended CAdES signatures (BES, EPES, T, A)
  - Augmentation to C, X, XL, A forms

### Part 4: Testing Conformance of CAdES baseline signatures
- **Reference**: ETSI TS 119 124-4 [i.4]
- **Basis**: Against ETSI EN 319 122-1 [i.1]
- **Classification of requirements**:
  1. Requirements specific to CAdES baseline signatures
  2. Requirements common to both baseline (EN 319 122-1 [i.1]) and extended (EN 319 122-2 [i.2])

### Part 5: Testing Conformance of extended CAdES signatures
- **Reference**: ETSI TS 119 124-5 [i.8]
- **Basis**: Against ETSI EN 319 122-2 [i.2]
- **Classification of requirements**:
  1. Requirements common to both baseline and extended (incorporated by reference to ETSI TS 119 124-4 [i.4])
  2. Requirements specific to extended CAdES signatures
