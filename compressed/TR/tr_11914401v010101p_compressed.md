# ETSI TR 119 144-1: Electronic Signatures and Infrastructures (ESI); PAdES digital signatures - Testing Conformance and Interoperability; Part 1: Overview
**Source**: ETSI | **Version**: V1.1.1 | **Date**: 2016-06 | **Type**: Informative
**Original**: DTR/ESI-0019144-1

## Scope (Summary)
This document provides an overview of the multi-part deliverable covering test suites for testing interoperability and conformance of PAdES signatures. It describes the general set and lists the features of each test suite for PAdES baseline and additional signatures. The document is for information only; normative requirements are in other parts.

## 4 Testing Conformance and Interoperability
### 4.1 Part 2: Test suites for testing interoperability of PAdES baseline signatures
ETSI TS 119 144-2 [i.6] specifies test suites for interoperability of PAdES baseline signatures against ETSI EN 319 142-1 [i.1]. Four levels of baseline signatures are defined. Test suites are layered by level:
- B-B level conformance
- B-T level conformance
- B-LT level conformance
- B-LTA level conformance
- Augmentation from B-T to B-LTA level
- Negative baseline tests (B-B, B-T, B-LTA)

### 4.2 Part 3: Test suites for testing interoperability of additional PAdES signatures profiles
ETSI TS 119 144-3 [i.7] specifies test suites for interoperability of additional PAdES signatures against ETSI EN 319 142-2 [i.2]. Different signature forms are covered. Test suites layered by form:
- PAdES-E-BES
- PAdES-E-EPES
- PAdES-E-LTV
- XAdES signatures of XML documents embedded in PDF containers
- XAdES signatures on XFA forms
- Negative additional signatures (E-BES, E-EPES, E-LTV)

### 4.3 Part 4: Testing Conformance of PAdES baseline signatures
ETSI TS 119 144-4 [i.4] defines requirements for building software tools for testing technical conformity of PAdES baseline signatures against ETSI EN 319 142-1 [i.1]. Requirements are classified into two groups:
1) Requirements specific to PAdES baseline signatures.
2) Requirements common to both baseline (EN 319 142-1) and additional signatures (EN 319 142-2).

### 4.4 Part 5: Testing Conformance of additional PAdES signatures
ETSI TS 119 144-5 [i.8] defines requirements for building software tools for testing technical conformance of additional PAdES signatures against ETSI EN 319 142-2 [i.2]. Requirements are classified as:
1) Requirements common to both baseline and additional signatures (incorporated by reference to TS 119 144-4).
2) Requirements specific to additional PAdES signatures profiles.
