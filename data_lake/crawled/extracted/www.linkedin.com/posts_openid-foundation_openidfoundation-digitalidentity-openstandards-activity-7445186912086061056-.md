Track 6 at
#ID4Africa2026
: Ecosystems

How do countries build sovereign, scalable identity systems—and how do they evolve them into trusted, interoperable ecosystems?
This track explores two complementary pathways shaping Africa’s digital identity future:

✔️ Open Source (MOSIP, OpenCRVS, DHIS2) — enabling countries to build and operate national systems with greater control, adaptability, and sustainability
✔️ Open Standards (Verifiable Credentials & Digital Wallets) — enabling interoperability, decentralized trust, and cross-sector integration

These are not competing models—they are different architectural choices that shape how ecosystems emerge.

The conversation moves beyond technology to what really matters:
- governance
- sustainability
- trust at scale

Because the future of identity is not about standalone systems…
It is about ecosystems that create value.
